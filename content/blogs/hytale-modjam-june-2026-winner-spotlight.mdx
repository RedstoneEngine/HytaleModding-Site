The results of the Hytale x HytaleModding ModJam June 2026 have finally been announced! We'd like to take this opportunity to spotlight the well-deserved winners and reflect on this amazing event. 

This was Hytale's Third Modding Contest. The goals of this modding contest were very simple:

- Promote modding & creativity amongst modders
- Help the Hytale team discover more ways to improve their modding tools
- And lastly, to find incredible talent within the Hytale community to hire, regardless if they were finalists or not. 

We'd like to give a huge thank you to Hypixel Studios for partnering with us on this. It means a lot to us to work alongside Hypixel on something like this, with them funding the $5,000 prize pool and trusting HytaleModding to run submissions, moderation, and logistics so they could keep their focus on Hytale itself.
We'd also like to thank all of the judges involved in this two-stage process - our internal HytaleModding judges, who combed through every entry to narrow the field down to finalists, and the panel at the final judging stream: [BuddhaCat](https://x.com/ItsBuddhaCat), [RyanHCode](https://x.com/RyanHCode), [Ktar](https://x.com/Ktar5), [Neil](https://x.com/itsneildev), [Kweebec Corner](https://x.com/KweebecCorner), and [Kaupenjoe](https://x.com/Kaupenjoe). 

Finally, a MASSIVE thank you to all of the modders who poured their hearts and souls into their projects and submitted such incredible mods. We appreciate every single one of you.

## Final Thoughts
As the chapter of this modjam comes to an end, we want to thank everyone who participated, whether you were a finalist or not. The mods submitted were incredible, and we will continue trying our best to support the modding community and help modders get their work out there. 

<b>We are very grateful to Hypixel Studios for trusting us to run this event, and we hope to continue working with them in the future. This is just the beginning of what we hope will be a long and fruitful partnership between HytaleModding and Hypixel Studios.</b>

We are incredibly excited for what's to come, and we hope to see you all in the next ModJam!
